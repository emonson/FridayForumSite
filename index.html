Purpose

The Visualization Friday Forum seminar series is a forum for faculty, staff and students from across the university (and beyond Duke) to share their research involving the development and/or application of visualization methodologies. Our goal is to build an interdisciplinary community of visualization experts whose combined knowledge can facilitate research and promote innovation. Anyone is welcome to attend.

Contact

If you wish to receive email announcements each week, please subscribe to the Visualization mailing list. To do so, email sympa@duke.edu and put "SUBSCRIBE visualization" in the body of the message. The email account that you use to send this note will be the account added to the sympa email list.

For more information contact Eric Monson (emonson {at} duke.edu) or Angela Zoss (angela {dot} zoss {at} duke.edu)
